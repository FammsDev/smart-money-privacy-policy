6. Permissions We Use and Why

  • CAMERA: The app uses your device's camera to let you take photos of receipts or other images for expense tracking. These images are stored locally and are not shared without your consent.
  • INTERNET: The app requires internet access for syncing data, loading advertisements, and enabling in-app purchases.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE & READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: These permissions are needed to access images from your device that you select for expense tracking. The app does not access or store other files on your device without explicit action from you.
  • VIBRATE: The app uses vibration to provide haptic feedback for specific actions, such as confirming user actions.

Note: The app does not collect or share personal information without your explicit consent. The permissions requested are necessary for the app to function as intended and provide the best user experience.

Cloud Backups: Limited in number; older backups are automatically deleted.

Account Data: Retained while your account is active.
